# Solar Installation Landing Page

High-converting solar landing page built for U.S. solar installation companies focused on generating qualified homeowner leads.

## What This Landing Page Does

* Captures high-intent solar leads through a multi-step qualification form
* Reduces bounce rates with mobile-first UX and fast-loading design
* Builds trust using testimonials, financing messaging, FAQ handling, and conversion-focused structure
* Guides visitors toward one action: requesting a solar savings estimate

## Lead Generation Features

* Multi-step homeowner qualification quiz
* Zip code targeting
* Electricity bill qualification
* Timeline filtering for serious buyers
* Mobile-optimized conversion flow
* Fast lead submission handling

## Instant Lead Notifications

When a visitor submits the form:

* Lead details are instantly delivered by email
* Sales teams can contact prospects quickly
* Homeowner qualification data is included in the submission
* Designed for fast response workflows

## Built For

* Solar installation companies
* Local lead generation businesses
* Paid advertising campaigns
* Facebook Ads / Google Ads traffic
* Home service businesses focused on conversions

* Hero offer
* Trust section

## Goal

Turn paid traffic into qualified solar consultation leads.
